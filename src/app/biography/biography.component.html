About Me

My name is Nicholas Martinez, I am 22 years old and a budding software developer! I graduated Summa Cum Laude from the Rutgers University School of Arts and Sciences in May 2021. I started to take a great interest in Computer Science around my junior year of high school when I took an AP Computer Science course, and my interest has grown ever since. I have implemented many diverse applications using Java, C, Objective-C, Angular, and Typescript, which you can check out on my Projects page!

After high school was over, I decided to get my Associate's degree at Mercer County Community College. My experience there was a good stepping stone for what Rutgers would be like, and the decreased workload allowed me to work on some pet projects and hone my skills as a programmer.

Throughout my time at Mercer, I found a part time job at a small software development company called Visionware Systems. I started out as a tester, and as time went on they allowed me work on their projects. This was incredibly helpful as it helped me gain real world experience and get a feel for what code bases would potentially be like at other companies.

I graduated Summa Cum Laude from the Rutgers University School of Arts and Sciences in New Brunswick, NJ in May 2021. The Computer Science curriculum has a multitude of courses that I feel has greatly enriched my knowledge of Software Engineering, and has furthered my interest in programming as a whole. I look forward to working at great and exciting companies!
