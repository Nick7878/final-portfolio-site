About Me

My name is Nicholas Martinez, I am 21 years old and a programmer who is currently studying Computer Science at Rutgers University. I started to take a great interest in Computer Science around my junior year of high school when I took an AP Computer Science course, and my interest has grown ever since. I have made a good amount of applications in java, and some websites which you can check out on the Projects page!

After high school was over, I decided to get my Associate's degree at Mercer County Community College. My experience there was a good stepping stone for what Rutgers would be like, and the decreased workload allowed me to work on some pet projects and hone my skills as a programmer.

Throughout my time at Mercer, I managed to find a part time job at a small software development company called Visionware Systems. I started out as a tester, then as time went on, they allowed me work on their projects. This was incredibly helpful, as it helped me gain real world experience and get a feel for what code bases would potentially be like at other companies.

As of right now, I am currently attending Rutgers University in New Brunswick where I hope to get my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. So far my experience here has taught me to be a great problem solver, and has furthered my interest in programming as a whole.
